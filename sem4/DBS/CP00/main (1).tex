\subsection*{\textbf{Theme:} \normalfont{Sports Complex Database}}

\subsection*{Motivation: \normalfont{The motivation is to simplify and
accelerate the daily operations of the sports complex, solve issues with scheduling conflicts,
payment transactions, and monitoring the use of individual sports facilities.}} 

\subsection*{Short Description: \normalfont{This project focuses on the design and implementation of a database system
for managing reservations in the sports complex, which includes  group/solo trainings with trainer or without. The goal is to effectively digitize
the process of reservations, payments, reservation collisions etc.}} 


\subsection*{Taxonomic and Operational Scope: \normalfont{The proposed system will include several taxonomic levels (records of branches,
sports halls, clients, trainers and trainings) and will handle operational data such as attendance at training sessions, payments.}} 


\subsection*{\textbf{Bonus:} \normalfont{Detailed List of Entities and Relationships}}


\subsection*{\textbf{Entities(WHO and WHAT):}}
1. \textbf{Branch} - \textbf{\textcolor{blue}{\underline{address}}}(city, street, house number) \newline
2. \textbf{Sports Hall} - \textbf{\textcolor{blue}{\underline{name}}}, size, specialization \newline
3. \textbf{User} - \textbf{\textcolor{blue}{\underline{login}}}, password \newline
4. \textbf{Client} (derives from User) - preferences in sports \newline
5. \textbf{Trainer} (derives from User) - specialization, education \newline
6. \textbf{Personal Data} - \textbf{\textcolor{blue}{\underline{name}}, \textcolor{blue}{\underline{surname}}, \textcolor{blue}{\underline{date of birth}}, \textcolor{red}{\underline{e-mail}}, \textcolor{green}{\underline{phone number}}} \newline
7. \textbf{Reservation} - status, reservation date \newline
8. \textbf{Payment} - amount, payment date, payment method \newline
9. \textbf{Training} - type, training date \newline

\subsection*{\textbf{Relationships(What does):}}
1. Branch \textbf{contains} Sports Halls \newline
2. User \textbf{has} Personal data \newline
3. Client \textbf{makes} Reservation \newline
4. Client \textbf{makes} Payment \newline
5. Trainer \textbf{is assistant for }trainer \newline
6. Trainer \textbf{conducts} trainings \newline
7. Reservation \textbf{includes} Payment \newline
8. Reservation \textbf{applies to} Training \newline
9. Training \textbf{takes place in} Sports hall \newline
